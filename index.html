ABOUT US

The Leo Club of the University of Ruhuna, situated in the scenic campus of the University in Sri Lanka, is a dynamic student organization dedicated to service and leadership. Comprised of diverse and dedicated students, their commitment extends to making a positive impact on both campus life and the surrounding community.

Through a variety of projects, from education to environmental conservation, the Leos exemplify holistic development.Serving as a training ground for future leaders, the club fosters qualities of teamwork, empathy, and initiative. Regular meetings and collaborative events create an environment where students can harness their potential while contributing meaningfully to societal welfare.
